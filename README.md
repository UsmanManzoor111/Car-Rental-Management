# Car-Rental-Management

A car rental management system is a software application that helps car rental companies manage their operations, including vehicle reservations, rental agreements, customer information. The system automates many manual processes involved in car rental operations, providing a streamlined and efficient way to manage the rental process from start to finish. It includes features such as online reservations, customer accounts and contact us form . The system is designed to help rental companies manage their fleets, improve customer service, increase operational efficiency, and ultimately, maximize revenue.
